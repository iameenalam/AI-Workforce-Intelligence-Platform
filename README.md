# ReeOrg

**ReeOrg** is an advanced **Business and Workforce Intelligence Platform** built for the **Post-AI Era**, empowering HR leaders with real-time clarity and data-driven insights to proactively manage workforce transformation. Designed for CHROs, HR Directors, and HR Business Partners, ReeOrg simplifies the visualization of organizational structure, identifies talent-related risks, and streamlines future workforce planningâ€”all in a single, intuitive platform.

## Features

- **Organizational Visualization**  
  Real-time interactive visualization of organizational structure, roles, reporting lines, skills, and capabilities for comprehensive workforce insight.

- **Risk Intelligence & Analytics**  
  Identification of hidden risks such as attrition, role redundancy, AI disruption, and skill decay to enable proactive workforce risk management.

- **Workforce Simulation & Planning**  
  Simulation tools to test workforce redesigns, model organizational changes, and plan upskilling strategies aligned with business outcomes.

- **Actionable Dashboards**  
  Intuitive dashboards provide workforce intelligence and insights to support data-driven decisions and transformation initiatives.

- **Future-proof Strategy Enablement**  
  Built to help HR leaders align talent with strategy, future-proof roles, and confidently lead organizational change.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js  
- **Database**: MongoDB  
- **AI Intelligence**: OpenAI  
- **Styling & Animations**: Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion  
- **Asset Management**: Cloudinary
